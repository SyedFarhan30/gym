How to Do Incline Bench Press: Muscles Worked & Proper Form

Muscles Worked in the Incline Bench Press

Primary muscles worked:

  • Chest
  • Front Deltoid

Secondary muscles worked:

  • Triceps

How to Incline Bench Press

  1. Sitting on an inclined bench, unrack a barbell and hold it on straight arms above your shoulders.
  2. Inhale and lower the bar down to your chest.
  3. Press the bar up to straight arms while exhaling.

Commentary

The incline bench press is a mixture of the regular bench press and the overhead press, and both the front deltoids and the upper portions of the chest muscles are trained in this exercise.
